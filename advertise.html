Advertise With MetroFeed

MetroFeed is an independent, real-time transit and info platform currently in early growth. We offer rotating banner ad placement at the top of every page (excluding legal or static pages like this one). Ads are shown to local audiences within each city — current availability is Portland only. Pricing is determined by an in-house algorithm based on the average number of daily users over the last 7 days. We can provide a receipt and breakdown on request.

Ad Placement Details

  • Banner Format: Static image only (.PNG), 728x90 pixels
  • Placement: City-wide top banner (appears on most pages within a specific city)
  • Availability: Daily packages from 1 to 7 days. Requests beyond 7 days require direct discussion.
  • Individual page ads: Not currently available.
  • Payments: All payments are handled via PayPal and must be completed before an ad is placed.
